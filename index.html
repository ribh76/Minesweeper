Minesweeper by Rishi Bhat

How this game works: 1) Click a space (Preferably a corner) and you should see a number pop up, denoting the number of mines, in a 1 space radius. 2) right click to flag, left click to reveal a space. 3) if the numbers turn bold, you have stepped on a mine. the game is over. 4) Flag all the mines to win! 5) good luck XD
